Dr. Heron: Me(lol)

Hello, I am Santino Gabriel A. Manalili but you can call me Dr. Heron. I am the proud owner of this peaceful and calm sanctuary, the Bird Watch. I have always had a fascination with birds since I was a kid. An interaction that I remember was my grandfather owning this eagle statue. This eagle statue was quite fierce-looking and scared me as a kid. Usually, my grandfather would scare me into doing chores by telling me that the eagle would fly the "naughty boys" away. This, as horrifying as it was, piqued my interest on birds. However, I lost interest in birds as fast as they piqued my interest. However, in the past grade, I gained interest in birds again. This was because I had discovered the second-greatest thing to crustacean memes: cockatoo videos. After this discovery, I decided to look into cockatoos and found out that they were hard to take care of because of their bordeline obssession to be near someone. This kind of resonated with me since I was feeling lonely at the time and I didn't have many friends to talk to. My goal with this website is to have people to be able to connect with birds.
